About OSG

OSG stands for Online Survey Generator.

It lets every registerd user to generate their own survey and publish it on the site.

Any other user is allowed to vote in all available surveys. Results are displeyed as a bar chart.

You can use search form to find the survey you want by its title, creator or the date it was added to the database.

All deleted surveys are archived, but still available for result check.

Please, respect others and do not publish offensive content.

And remeber to have fun!
